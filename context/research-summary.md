# Research Summary: Gilet Bandit Chocolat
## 1. Product Overview
- **Name:** Gilet Bandit Chocolat (Bandit Cardigan)
- **Brand:** Rézine Paris
- **Price:** 52,50 € (Discounted from 75,00 €)
- **Material:** 40% Viscose, 20% Wool, 10% Cashmere, 20% Polyamide
- **Origin:** Woven and printed in France
- **Vibe:** "Capsule Cavale" - Modern Day Cowboy, Folk, Boho-Chic, Parisian Ease.

## 2. Dream Customer (Avatar)
- **Name:** "The Urban Bohemian" (Elodie)
- **Desires:** Wants to look effortlessly stylish ("allure libre"), values comfort ("réconfortant"), seeks unique pieces that aren't mass-market basics.
- **Fears:** Being cold in transition months, looking "stiff" or "boring", wearing cheap synthetic knits that pill or itch.
- **Values:** Made in France, natural materials (wool/cashmere), distinctive prints.

## 3. Pain Points & Solutions
| Pain Point | The Bandit Solution |
| :--- | :--- |
| "Knits are either itchy wool or cheap acrylic." | **The 'Cloud-Soft' Blend**: 40% Viscose + 10% Cashmere softens the 20% Wool. |
| "Winter clothes look bulky and unstylish." | **Structure without Constraint**: Wide collar and mid-hip cut structure the silhouette. |
| "I look like everyone else in plain sweaters." | **The Bandana Statement**: A bold, vintage-inspired print that stands out. |
| "It's hard to dress for 'in-between' weather." | **The Perfect Weight**: Warm enough for autumn, light enough to layer. |

## 4. The Big Domino
"The problem isn't that comfortable clothes are ugly; it's that you haven't found the *right* blend of heritage print and luxury fabric. Once you wear the Bandit, you'll realize you can have the 'Wild West' spirit with Parisian refinement."

## 5. The 3 Secrets (Hooks)
- **Secret 1 (Vehicle): The "Bandana-Chic" Mechanism**
  - *Old Way:* Boring, solid-color cardigans from fast fashion.
  - *New Way:* A statement piece that transforms a simple jeans-and-tee outfit into a "look."
- **Secret 2 (Internal): The "No-Itch" Cashmere Promise**
  - *False Belief:* "Wool implies itching."
  - *Truth:* The specific viscose/cashmere ratio creates a barrier of softness while keeping the warmth of wool.
- **Secret 3 (External): The "Made in France" Scarcity**
  - *False Belief:* "It's too expensive for a cardigan."
  - *Truth:* You are paying for French craftsmanship and a limited "Capsule Cavale" drop, not mass-produced waste.

## 6. Founder Story (Epiphany Bridge - Fictionalized for Protocol)
- **Backstory:** We loved the spirit of the American West—the freedom, the bandana prints, the ruggedness.
- **Wall:** But traditional western wear was stiff, heavy, and often uncomfortable for city life. We wanted that "cowboy cool" but with the touch of a Parisian atelier.
- **Epiphany:** We realized we could take the *pattern* of the west and apply it to the *finest French knitwear* techniques.
- **Plan:** We blended 10% Cashmere for luxury, Viscose for drape, and Wool for warmth. We found a workshop in France to weave it.
- **Transformation:** The Gilet Bandit was born. It's not just a sweater; it's a mood. "Allure libre."
