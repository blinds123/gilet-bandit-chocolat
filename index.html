    I'm obsessed with the 'Americana' look—vintage bandanas, worn-in denim. But living in Paris, I couldn't walk into a meeting wearing a dusty old thrift store find. I needed polish.

    Every 'luxury' knit I found was boring. Beige, grey, black. snooze. Where was the personality? And every 'cool' print I found was printed on cheap, sweaty acrylic.

    Then I realized I was asking the wrong question. I was asking 'Where can I find this?' when I should have been asking 'How can I make this?' What if I took the rebellious spirit of the Wild West bandana and forced it to grow up?

    I tracked down a French atelier that works with luxury houses. I begged them to weave a complex bandana pattern using a specific blend: 10% Cashmere (for luxe softness), 40% Viscose (for liquid drape), and 20% Wool (for real heat).
